Hey friends!

We designed the app aiming to provide you interesting and useful travel information. Hope it helps to make your trips bright and remarkable.
Besides country information we also built in MapsWithMe offline maps API which allows locating travel guide's places on the offline maps.
If you have any ideas how we can improve the app, or you need additional information, don't hesitate to contact us at support@guidewithme.com.
Please like our Facebook page, where we gather our friends to share travel news and tips.
Thanks for using UK GuideWithMe!
